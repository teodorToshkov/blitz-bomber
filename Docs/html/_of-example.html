Holds information about what happents when the Floor is destroyed, can be called from other scripts.
We set that we have destroyed the Floor and invoke the base Destroy method after a given time.
We decrease the Time.timeScale by the appropriate factor.
Depending on the numberOfFloorsDestroyed we decrease by a different factor.
Time.timeScale *= 1 - (slowDownRate / numberOfFloorsDestroyed) how TimeSlowerFloorScript.Destroy works If we have a slowDownRate equal to 0.5f and 1 active TimeSlowerFloorScript's and we Destroy another one, we will decrease the Time.timeScale by a factor of 1 - (0.5f / 2) = 1 - 0.25f = 0.75f. And therefore the overal Time.timeScale will be 0.5f * 0.75f = 0.375f.
